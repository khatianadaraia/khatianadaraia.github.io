title: "Childhood Nurturance Capital and Working Memory in Later Adulthood"
collection: publications
category: workingpapers
permalink: /publication/nurturance-capital
date: 2024-01-01
---

This paper examines how childhood nurturance capital, the internalised experience of attentive and supportive caregiving, shapes working memory capacity in later adulthood among individuals aged 50 and over, using longitudinal data from the U.S. Health and Retirement Study (HRS).
